## 1. Galileo — Heliocentrism Case

- **Case Identifier**: Galileo-Heliocentrism
- **Initial Constraint Set**:
  - Scriptural cosmology as the top-level truth constraint
  - Ecclesiastical authority as the final arbiter of epistemic validity
- **New Information / Structural Input**:
  - Telescopic observations supporting the heliocentric model
- **Constraint Inheritance Failure Point**:
  - Observability and reproducibility constraints failed to inherit into the theological judgment system
- **Post-failure Handling Path**:
  - CIF-d
- **Substitution Frame**:
  - Truth evaluation → heresy / order-violation frame
- **Outcome**:
  - Theory rejection, personal punishment
- **Meta Note**:
  - Not a science–religion conflict, but constraint priority fixation
