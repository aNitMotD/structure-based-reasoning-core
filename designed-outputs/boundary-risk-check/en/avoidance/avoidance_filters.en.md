# Observed Filtering Phrases

The phrases below are commonly observed during boundary risk checks.
They do not indicate correctness or failure.
They serve only as reference signals that a check may have been closed prematurely.

## Avoidance

- "Handled according to the guideline"
- "No procedural issue found"
- "Marked as requirement-compliant"
- "Out of scope for this check"
- "Passed as defined"
